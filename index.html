This project is simply a look at the voices of the internet. A summary, if you will. I love this crazy place for the people it brings me into contact with-- it is relationships that make the internet great, after all.

Yet, the negative side of things is frequently the more visible, the loudest, the scariest. We lose sight of the tender moments and human nuances. Here, I've started with the negative voices because they are so often foremost, so be warned. However, there is hope.

Without further ado, my tribute to the voices of the internet...
